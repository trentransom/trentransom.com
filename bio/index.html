Trent Ransom

Musical Life

Trent Ransom was recently a graduate student at The Juilliard School studying viola performance. Trent studied with Carol Rodland. Previous teachers include I-Hao Lee, Cyrus Beroukhim, and Teri Einfeldt. Trent has attended Green Mountain Chamber Music Festival, I-Hao Lee Program (China), Round Top Festival Orchestra, Boston University Tanglewood Institute, and Preludio Program Ithaca College Suzuki Institute. In 2020 Trent's string quartet was chosen as a featured performer in the DePaul School of Music Spring Gala. Trent was accepted to the Chicago Civic Orchestra for the 2021 season. Honors include winning the Connecticut Youth Symphony concerto competition, and a prize at the Hartford Musical Club Competition.

Beyond Music

Trent enjoys outdoor activities. He has biked accross the country from Charleston, South Carolina to Santa Monica, California and he has also hiked the John Muir Trail. He also enjoys playing chess and Mario Kart.

Software Engineering

Having left music Trent has turned his attention to software engineering. He is at work in the FinTech Bootcamp at Columbia University. He just finished the first project of the program and is excelling in the course. During this time Trent is also teaching himself web design. He has built this website himself from scratch.
